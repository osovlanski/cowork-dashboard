## ✈️ Weekend Travel Idea

Only ~1.5 hours from Tel Aviv. The grottos at Rosh HaNikra are genuinely stunning — the cable car, the turquoise sea caves, the border cliffs. Pair it with a lazy lunch in Nahariya (solid beach town, very chill vibe). Could be a Friday day trip or a Saturday outing with a friend.

- 18:00 — Home, tired and happy

*Perfect for the end of April — before the serious summer heat kicks in.*
