## 🔨 DIY Project This Week

**Project: Window Insulation Film 🪟☀️**

May in Tel Aviv — the heat is just starting. This is the perfect week to get the reflective window film up before the apartment becomes a greenhouse in June. It's practical, quick, and you'll feel the difference immediately.

- **Time:** 1.5–2 hours per window (do 1–2 windows this Saturday)
- **Budget:** ₪300–500 total for 3–4 windows
- **Get:** Window film (reflective or privacy, your choice), spray bottle, squeegee, utility knife
- **Where to buy:** Ace Hardware or Obi — they carry it ready-to-go

This is one of those projects with immediate ROI: lower AC costs all summer, less screen glare during work-from-home days, and your furniture won't fade. Start with the window that gets the most direct afternoon sun.
